For those of you that were unable to make it, we thought of you and took a few notes for ya.

On of the more interesting ideas discussed, was the idea that us Web professionals do not always have the time to create or update our own sites. Obviously this is a major short-coming, but one that makes sense. After all, our sites don't pay us—but our clients do.

A suggestion was to have others from our group help their fellow peers on their downtime and vice-versa. In addition to building experience and peer relationships, this is a great chance to add something easily to your portfolio.
